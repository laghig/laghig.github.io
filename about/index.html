About me

I'm a recent ETH graduate in Management, Technology, & Economics with a background in Environmental Engineering.
Previously, I worked at the Swiss stock exchange in the Index team.
I am particularly interested in projects at the intersection of sustainability and data analysis. During my university years I worked on a model that used natural language processing and machine learning to predict the environmental footprint of food products. Moreover, I estimated permafrost degradation in the Canadian High Arctic by comparing digital elevation models acquired through remote sensing observations over time.
